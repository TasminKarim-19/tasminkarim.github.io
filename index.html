About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science & Engineering at Oakland University (Michigan, USA). My research focuses on software security with emphasis on LLMs for code, vulnerability datasets, and graph‑based context (AST/CFG/PDG). I enjoy building practical pipelines that connect program analysis tools with deep learning to improve detection and explanation of vulnerabilities.
